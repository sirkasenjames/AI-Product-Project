# The Prototype Bet

## What I Built
<!-- One sentence: what does this prototype demonstrate? -->
An AI-powered booking assistant prototype that helps clients seamlessly book and reschedule appointments with service-based solopreneurs through conversational calendar management.

## Tool Used
<!-- v0 / Cursor / Lovable / other -->
Lovable

## Prototype Link
<!-- Paste the shareable URL -->
https://bookful-ai-assist.lovable.app

## AI Value Archetype
<!-- Automator / Copilot / Oracle / Creator / Orchestrator -->
Automator (primary): AI handles scheduling workflows automatically

Copilot (secondary): AI assists users in choosing optimal appointment times

## The Bet in One Sentence
<!-- What you're building, for whom, why now -->
I believe AI can dramatically reduce the friction of booking and rescheduling appointments for service-based solopreneurs, increasing completed bookings and client satisfaction at a time when consumers increasingly expect instant, conversational, self-serve experiences.

## Kill Criteria
<!-- When would you stop? What evidence would kill this bet? -->
If users consistently prefer traditional calendar interfaces over AI-assisted scheduling, or if AI-driven booking flows fail to meaningfully improve booking completion rates or customer satisfaction versus existing tools, we would stop pursuing this direction.
